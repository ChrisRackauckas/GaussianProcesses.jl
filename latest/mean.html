    Means

    GaussianProcesses.MeanConst — Type.
    MeanConst <: Mean

    Constant mean function

    \[m(x) = β\]

    with constant $β$.

    source
    GaussianProcesses.MeanLin — Type.
    MeanLin <: Mean

    Linear mean function

    \[m(x) = xᵀβ\]

    with linear coefficients $β$.

    source
    GaussianProcesses.MeanPoly — Type.
    MeanPoly <: Mean

    Polynomial mean function

    \[m(x) = ∑ᵢⱼ βᵢⱼxᵢʲ\]

    with polynomial coefficients $βᵢⱼ$ of shape $d × D$ where $d$ is the dimension of observations and $D$ is the degree of the polynomial.

    source
